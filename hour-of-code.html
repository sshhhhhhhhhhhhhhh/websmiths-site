About the Event

The Hour of Code event organized by WebSmiths introduced programming to young students in a fun and interactive way. Our team aimed to inspire the next generation of innovators by teaching them the basics of coding through engaging activities.


If you want to watch the whole event, check out the video, instagram, and facebook posts. If you like the video, don't forget to drop a like and comment to share your impressions!

Event Highlights

Introducing ADA University

Students were welcomed with an introduction to ADA University and its values.

Understanding Algorithms

Basic concepts of algorithms were introduced through examples and puzzles.

Interactive Programming

Students engaged in block-based coding exercises to solve challenges.

Diverse Participation

Over 25 students from different backgrounds joined the event.

Certificates of Completion

All participants received certificates to commemorate their experience.
