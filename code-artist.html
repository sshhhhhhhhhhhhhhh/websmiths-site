The density of the lines controls the tightness of the star, resulting in a visually stunning radial pattern.

The Spiral algorithm creates a flowing spiral pattern by incrementally increasing the radius while rotating around the center. The angle increment and radius growth determine the tightness and coverage of the spiral. This algorithm offers a dynamic and motion-like visual effect.

The Wave algorithm generates a dynamic sinusoidal wave pattern across the canvas. The amplitude determines the height of the crests and troughs, while the frequency defines the number of waves stretching across the width. This algorithm blends the rhythmic motion of waves with the image, resulting in a smooth and energetic visual effect.

The Radial Burst algorithm creates an explosion-like pattern radiating outward from the center. By generating evenly spaced lines at multiple angles, the algorithm produces a sharp, dynamic burst effect. The number of rays and their thickness can be adjusted to craft anything from subtle bursts to powerful explosions, making it a versatile addition to the visual repertoire.

üå≥ Fractal Tree Algorithm

The Fractal Tree algorithm creates a recursive branching pattern that emulates the growth of a natural tree. Starting from a single trunk, each branch splits into smaller branches at specified angles and lengths, forming a complex and visually captivating fractal structure. By adjusting parameters like branch length and angle, you can generate a wide variety of tree shapes, adding depth and organic beauty to your art.

üé® Pixel Art Algorithm

The Pixel Art algorithm transforms your image into a retro-style mosaic by breaking it into large square blocks, each filled with a single averaged color. The size of the blocks, or "pixels," can be adjusted to create a detailed or abstract aesthetic. This algorithm adds a playful and nostalgic touch, reminiscent of classic 8-bit and 16-bit video game art styles which makes it a simple yet effective visual transformation.
